About Hotel Blue Moon

Amid shops on a side street, this unfussy budget hotel is an 8-minute walk from the Firoz Shah Palace Complex and 1 km from Green Square Market. Hisar Junction railway station is 2 km away.

Simply furnished rooms with bright accent walls feature flat-screen TVs and air conditioning, along with en suite wet rooms. Room service is available.

The hotel offers a comfortable and peaceful stay with modern essentials, ideal for families, couples, and travelers seeking convenience and quality hospitality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability.

Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we provide free parking facilities for our guests.

Do you offer airport/railway station pickup?

We can arrange pickup services on request. Please contact us in advance to arrange transportation.

Is Wi-Fi available in rooms?

Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and common areas.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel. Please inform us in advance if you're traveling with pets.
